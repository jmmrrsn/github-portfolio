About

I'm a Norwich-based graphic and digital designer with 15 years' experience, also working in London. I specialise in print, digital, motion, and web design and have led multidisciplinary creative teams. I love solving design challenges, keeping the process clear, collaborative and jargon-free for clients across a wide range of industries.
